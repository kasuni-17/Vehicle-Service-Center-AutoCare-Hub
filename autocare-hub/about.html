Who We Are

Built on Trust & Excellence

AutoCare Hub was founded with one simple mission: to provide the highest quality vehicle care services in Sri Lanka at fair and transparent prices.

What started as a small garage in Colombo has grown into a full-service automotive care center trusted by thousands of customers across the Western Province. We treat every vehicle as if it were our own.

Our team of certified technicians and detailing specialists bring passion and precision to every job — whether it's a routine oil change or a complete vehicle transformation.
